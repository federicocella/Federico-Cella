About
My name is Federico, I'm 20 and I'm currently studying Communication Design in Milan. I've always been passionate and fascinated by almost everything creative you can do with a computer, may it be video editing, programming, page layouts, graphics and so on. What strikes me most about these things is the ability of the artworks to tell a story even if they have no words in them. I also like stories a lot and I spend most of the time left from university reding books and watching movies or TV series.
